Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti – A classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, red pepper flakes, and fresh parsley. It's quick, flavorful, and perfect for a simple yet delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced garlic and sauté until golden brown, being careful not to burn it.
  4. Stir in the red pepper flakes, then add the drained spaghetti.
  5. Toss the pasta in the oil, adding reserved pasta water if needed for moisture.
  6. Season with salt, stir in chopped parsley, and mix well.
  7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
